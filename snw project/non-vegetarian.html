Classic Chicken Curry

A simple and authentic chicken curry that's full of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 (14 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup water or chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pan and sauté onion until golden.
  2. Add garlic and ginger and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Stir in curry powder, then add the chicken pieces. Cook until lightly browned.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes and water. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.

Spaghetti Bolognese

A rich and meaty sauce served over spaghetti, a family favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, cook ground beef, onion, and carrots until meat is browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, oregano, and red wine (if using).
  3. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and let it cook for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
  5. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti.
