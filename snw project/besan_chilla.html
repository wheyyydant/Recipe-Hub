Here is the quick, non-table recipe for Besan Ka Chilla (Savory Chickpea Pancakes).Besan Ka Chilla (Savory Chickpea Pancakes)This is a popular, protein-rich North Indian breakfast or snack made from chickpea flour (besan).1Key IngredientsDry: 1 cup Chickpea Flour (Besan), a pinch of Turmeric powder, a pinch of Carom Seeds (ajwain), salt, and red chili powder to taste.Wet: Approximately $\frac{3}{4}$ cup water (or more/less to achieve a thin, smooth batter), 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic paste.Veggies (optional): 2 tbsp finely chopped onion, tomato, capsicum (bell pepper), and fresh cilantro.Cooking: Oil for cooking.Short Recipe StepsMake Batter: In a bowl, whisk together the chickpea flour, water, salt, and all the dry spices (turmeric, carom seeds, chili powder) until you have a smooth, pourable consistency, similar to a thin crepe batter.Add Flavor: Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and the finely chopped onions and other vegetables (if using).Heat Pan: Heat a non-stick griddle or tawa over medium-high heat and lightly grease it with oil.Pour & Spread: Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the hot pan. Quickly use the back of the ladle to spread the batter outwards in a thin, even circle (like a crepe).2Cook: Drizzle a little oil around the edges of the chilla. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the base is set and golden brown.Flip: Flip the chilla and cook the other side until lightly browned and cooked through.Serve immediately with green chutney or ketchup.
