Chana Masala (Spiced Chickpea Curry)This is a hearty, flavorful North Indian curry that is naturally vegan.Key IngredientsAromatics & Oil: 1 tbsp oil, 1 tsp cumin seeds, 1 chopped onion, 1 tsp each minced ginger and garlic.Base: 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes, 1 can (400g) chickpeas (rinsed and drained), $\frac{1}{2}$ cup water.Spices: 1 tsp coriander powder, 1 tsp cumin powder, $\frac{1}{2}$ tsp turmeric powder, 1 tsp garam masala.Finishing: Juice of $\frac{1}{2}$ lemon, salt to taste, fresh cilantro for garnish.Short Recipe StepsSauté Aromatics: Heat the oil in a pan. Add the cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add the chopped onion and sauté until golden brown (about 5-7 minutes). Stir in the minced ginger and garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.Toast Spices: Reduce the heat and stir in the coriander, cumin, and turmeric powders. Cook this spice mixture for about 30 seconds.Build Gravy: Add the crushed tomatoes and cook, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens and the oil begins to separate from the gravy (about 5-8 minutes).Simmer: Stir in the rinsed chickpeas and $\frac{1}{2}$ cup of water. Bring the curry to a simmer and let it cook, uncovered, for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to fully combine.Finish: Stir in the garam masala and the fresh lemon juice. Taste and adjust the salt level.Garnish generously with chopped cilantro before serving hot with rice or bread.
