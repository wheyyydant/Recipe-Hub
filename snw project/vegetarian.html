Hearty Lentil Soup

A warm and filling soup that's both healthy and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brown lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté onion, carrots, and celery until soft.
  2. Add lentils, vegetable broth, and cumin.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 40-45 minutes, or until lentils are tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper before serving.

Easy Vegetable Curry

A fragrant and creamy curry you can make with any vegetables you have.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 (14 oz) can coconut milk
  • 3 cups mixed vegetables (e.g., broccoli, cauliflower, bell peppers)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pan, heat coconut oil and sauté onion and garlic.
  2. Stir in curry powder and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add coconut milk and mixed vegetables.
  4. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15-20 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.
  5. Season with salt and serve over rice.
