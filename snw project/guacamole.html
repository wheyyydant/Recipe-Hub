Classic GuacamoleThis recipe is simple, vegan, and focuses on fresh, bold flavor.1Key IngredientsAvocados: 3 ripe avocados (Hass is recommended).2Acidity: 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice.Aromatics: $\frac{1}{4}$ cup finely diced red or white onion, 1 minced jalapeño (seeds removed for less heat).Freshness: $\frac{1}{4}$ cup finely chopped fresh cilantro.Seasoning: $\frac{1}{2}$ teaspoon salt (or to taste).Optional: $\frac{1}{2}$ medium tomato, seeded and diced.Short Recipe StepsPrep: Scoop the avocado flesh into a bowl.3Mash: Add the lime juice and salt to the avocado. Use a fork or potato masher to mash the mixture lightly until it reaches your desired consistency—keep it chunky for better texture!Mix: Stir in the diced onion, minced jalapeño, and chopped cilantro. If you are using tomato, gently fold it in last.Season: Taste the guacamole and adjust the salt or lime juice as needed.4 The final product should be tangy and well-seasoned.Serve immediately with tortilla chips or crunchy vegetables.
